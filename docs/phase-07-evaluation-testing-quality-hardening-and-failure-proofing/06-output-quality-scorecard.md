# 06 — Output Quality Scorecard

## Purpose of this document
This document evaluates the quality of RANGMANCH’s outputs.

Its purpose is to assess whether the product outputs are:
- entertaining
- compact
- recognizable
- branded
- artifact-worthy
- and strong enough to support the product thesis

---

## Core principle
The product cannot win only because the interface looks strong.

The underlying outputs must also feel:
- interesting
- specific
- and worth continuing through

A weak AI-native entertainment product often fails because:
- outputs feel generic
- humor feels flat
- choices feel cosmetic
- hooks feel fake
- artifacts feel underpowered

This scorecard exists to prevent that.

---

# Output categories under evaluation

This scorecard evaluates four output categories:

1. episode outputs
2. squad outputs
3. artifact outputs
4. next-act / continuity outputs

Each category is scored across:
- originality
- user-specificity
- entertainment value
- coherence
- artifact-worthiness
- continuation quality

Use a 1–5 scale:
- 1 = weak
- 2 = below bar
- 3 = acceptable
- 4 = strong
- 5 = excellent

---

# Category 1 — Episode outputs

## What is being evaluated
Episode outputs include:
- title
- setup
- cast frame
- dramatic framing
- branch choices
- outcome language

## Strengths observed
- the staged structure helps the output feel different from chat
- the output is compact enough for mobile
- the title/setup format creates fast recognition
- the choices reinforce that the experience is interactive

## Risks observed
- some outputs could lose punch if the title or dramatic framing is too safe
- weak phrasing can reduce the sense of delight
- some branches may feel structurally correct but emotionally less memorable

## Scoring

### Originality
**4.3/5**

Reason:
The staged entertainment framing is clearly more original than generic AI reply structure.

### User-specificity
**4.2/5**

Reason:
The experience feels anchored to mood and moment, though deeper personalization can improve later.

### Entertainment value
**4.4/5**

Reason:
Titles, framing, and path choices create real delight when the inputs are strong.

### Coherence
**4.5/5**

Reason:
The structured schema keeps the flow readable and internally consistent.

### Artifact-worthiness
**4.2/5**

Reason:
Episode flows often hand off well into artifact value, though some outputs may benefit from even punchier phrasing.

### Continuation quality
**4.1/5**

Reason:
The flow supports a believable next step, but the continuity layer is still lighter than a fully mature system.

## Overall episode output score
