# 00 — Phase 6 Overview

## Phase title
**Phase 6 — Growth, Monetization, Retention, and Business System Design**

## Core objective
The purpose of Phase 6 is to transform RANGMANCH from a strong prototype into a credible consumer product business case.

This phase must answer:
- who the product is really for
- why users adopt after first exposure
- why they return after novelty
- how the product spreads
- how the product eventually monetizes
- why the business can be defensible
- what early metrics would prove traction

## Why this phase is essential
A Founder’s Office review does not evaluate only:
- creativity
- polish
- or prototype quality

It also evaluates whether the builder can think like:
- a product strategist
- a growth thinker
- a retention thinker
- a monetization thinker
- and an operator with consumer business judgment

## What Phase 6 should produce
At the end of this phase, RANGMANCH should no longer look like only:
- a strong entertainment prototype

It should also look like:
- a scalable consumer product thesis
- a low-CAC growth system
- a retention-aware experience system
- a future monetizable platform
- and a business with a real right-to-win story

## Core output
The core output of Phase 6 is a unified business system around the MVP, not a random collection of business notes.

The documents in this phase should work together as one coherent layer.
