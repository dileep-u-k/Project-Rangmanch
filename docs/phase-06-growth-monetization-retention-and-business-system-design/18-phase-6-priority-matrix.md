## Final summary
The strongest Phase 6 story is not the broadest one.
It is the most focused one.

Emphasize what is already strongest in the MVP:
- adoption
- artifacts
- squad spread
- continuity
- and the cumulative moat story

That is the correct founder-grade prioritization.
