# Phase 6 — Growth, Monetization, Retention, and Business System Design

## Purpose
Phase 6 transforms Project RANGMANCH from a strong prototype into a credible consumer product business case.

This phase answers:
- who the product is really for
- why users adopt after first exposure
- why they return after novelty
- how the product spreads
- how it monetizes later
- why the business can be defensible
- what early metrics would prove traction

## Why this phase matters
Without this phase, RANGMANCH can look like:
- a creative student concept
- a polished entertainment prototype
- an interesting product without a real business engine

With this phase, RANGMANCH becomes:
- a category thesis
- a consumer behavior thesis
- a growth thesis
- a retention thesis
- a monetization thesis
- and a founder-grade strategic proposal

## Core workstreams
1. TAM and market framing
2. Adoption system design
3. Retention systems design
4. Growth loop architecture
5. Monetization strategy
6. Product economics thinking
7. Moat and right-to-win logic
8. Metrics framework
