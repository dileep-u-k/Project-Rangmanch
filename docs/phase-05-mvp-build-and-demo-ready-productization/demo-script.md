# Demo Script

## Goal
Present RANGMANCH as a companion-led entertainment product that turns daily life into interactive, portable, continuity-aware experiences.

---

## Flow A — Solo wow
1. Open onboarding
2. Enter nickname, vibe, humor, tone
3. Enter Home
4. Open Today’s Episode
5. Use demo example
6. Generate episode
7. Choose a path
8. Show outcome
9. Open artifact
10. Open next act

### Narration focus
- this is not chat-first
- the output is structured entertainment
- the result becomes portable
- continuity remains visible after the act

---

## Flow B — Social wedge
1. Back to Home
2. Open Squad Mode
3. Use demo example
4. Generate squad result
5. Show roles
6. Show verdict
7. Open artifact

### Narration focus
- the product understands group dynamics
- the result is compact and screenshot-worthy
- this supports near-zero-cost organic spread

---

## Flow C — Continuity proof
1. Open Next Act
2. Open Archive
3. Return Home

### Narration focus
- the world does not reset
- recent acts remain visible
- the product can support return and habit formation

---

## Presentation note
Keep the demo fast, clean, and emotionally legible.
Do not over-explain internal architecture during the product walk-through.
