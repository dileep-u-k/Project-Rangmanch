# 08 — Artifact and Share Screen Spec

## Purpose of this document
This document defines the Artifact / Share screen of Project RANGMANCH.

Its purpose is to specify:
- how generated value becomes visually portable
- what the artifact surface should show
- how the share moment should feel
- and how artifact presentation reinforces product identity

---

## Why this screen matters
Artifacts are one of the clearest proofs that RANGMANCH is not just a conversation interface.

The artifact screen proves:
- portability
- screenshot-worthiness
- emotional clarity
- growth plausibility
- product differentiation

If this screen is weak, the product feels less memorable and less shareable.

---

# Artifact screen required elements

## Element 1 — Hero artifact frame
Purpose:
- present the output as a branded entertainment object

Examples:
- episode poster
- verdict card
- chaos ranking
- squad result card
- next-act card

---

## Element 2 — Artifact headline hierarchy
Purpose:
- deliver quick emotional clarity
- communicate the result at a glance

Must feel:
- punchy
- compact
- branded
- screenshot-ready

---

## Element 3 — Supporting caption
Purpose:
- give context without clutter
- sharpen interpretation

Must remain:
- concise
- socially legible
- emotionally clean

---

## Element 4 — Action row
Purpose:
- expose the portable value clearly

Recommended actions:
- save
- share
- open next act
- back home

For MVP, these actions can be partly mocked.

---

# Design goals
The Artifact screen should feel:
- premium
- compact
- emotionally punchy
- visually distinctive
- screenshot-friendly

It should not feel like:
- raw text pasted into a box
- a generic social card
- an afterthought

---

# MVP implementation guidance
The prototype may use mocked artifact data as long as:
- the frame looks real
- the hierarchy is strong
- the screen communicates clear portable value

---

## Final summary
The Artifact / Share screen is where delight becomes a showable object.

Its job is to make the output feel:
- branded
- portable
- memorable
- and worth reacting to.
