# 18 — Phase 4 Signoff

## Purpose of this document
This document is the formal completion note for Phase 4 of Project RANGMANCH.

Its purpose is to summarize what has now been defined, confirm that the product now has a coherent UX/UI and prototype architecture foundation, and create a clean transition point into Phase 5.

---

## Phase title
**Phase 4 — UX/UI System, Interaction Design, and Prototype Architecture**

## Phase objective
The purpose of Phase 4 was to convert the product and intelligence design into a concrete prototype-ready experience system.

This phase was meant to answer:
- what exact screens exist
- what the user sees first
- how screens connect
- what the interaction patterns are
- how outputs are visually represented
- how the app feels moment to moment
- and how the intelligence design translates into a polished mobile experience

---
