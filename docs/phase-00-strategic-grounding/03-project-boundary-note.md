# 03 — Project Boundary Note

## Purpose of this document
This document defines exactly what Project RANGMANCH is, what it is not, what is in scope, and what is out of scope. Its purpose is to prevent category drift and protect the project from becoming a generic AI product.

## Core boundary principle
RANGMANCH is a **companion-led personal entertainment operating system** that transforms real-life user context into interactive entertainment.

Every later feature, screen, flow, prompt, and pitch statement should be judged against this truth.

---

## Section A — What RANGMANCH is

### 1. An AI-native entertainment companion
RANGMANCH is a product whose core behavior depends on AI. The AI is essential because it can:
- interpret mood and context
- personalize entertainment
- create dynamic episode structures
- sustain continuity
- frame social situations
- and maintain a living companion presence

### 2. A companion-led system
The product is organized around a recurring companion presence, not around a blank tool interface.
The companion acts as:
- narrator
- host
- director
- commentator
- chaos engine
- continuity keeper

### 3. A life-to-entertainment transformation system
The raw material of the product is the user’s real-life:
- moods
- moments
- boredom states
- social context
- friend-group dynamics
- celebrations
- chaos
- awkwardness
- everyday mini-events

The product transforms these into:
- episodes
- scenes
- mini-dramas
- squad verdicts
- artifacts
- and next-act hooks

### 4. An entertainment-first product
The center of gravity of the product is:
- delight
- play
- surprise
- drama
- expressive fun
- social energy
- repeatable entertainment

The product is not centered on emotional support, advice, or utility.

### 5. A short-session, mobile-native experience
RANGMANCH is designed for:
- fast entry
- quick delight
- repeat use
- simple emotional accessibility
- mobile-first behavior

It should fit real consumer usage patterns, not only long, effortful sessions.

### 6. A socially alive but not socially dependent system
The product should be strong for solo use, but become richer when social context is added.
Social value is an amplifier, not a mandatory dependency.

### 7. A continuity-rich experience
RANGMANCH should feel like a living entertainment world, not a one-time novelty machine.
The product should preserve:
- callbacks
- remembered style
- next-act logic
- active arcs
- returning companion presence

### 8. A consumer entertainment product, not a mere experiment
Even as an MVP, the product should feel like it could become:
- habit-forming
- socially spreading
- premium-enhanceable later
- and strategically defensible

---

## Section B — What RANGMANCH is not

### 1. Not a therapist-first product
It is not primarily for:
- mental health support
- emotional healing
- loneliness reduction
- dependence-driven companionship

### 2. Not a generic chatbot clone
It is not a product whose main loop is:
prompt -> answer

Open-ended chat is not the core product logic.

### 3. Not a Character AI or Replika style copy
It is not built around:
- fictional intimacy simulation
- romance-first interaction
- persona attachment as the primary value
- character-chat as the main loop

### 4. Not a social feed app
It is not organized around:
- endless scrolling
- public feed browsing
- content discovery as the main behavior
- creator-first feed economics

### 5. Not a casual game only
It may contain game-like interaction and choices, but it is not primarily:
- level-based
- mechanics-first
- leaderboard-first
- progression-economy-first

### 6. Not only a meme generator
RANGMANCH may create funny, sharable outputs, but it is much larger than a meme-creation tool.

### 7. Not a productivity or utility assistant
It is not meant to center:
- tasks
- scheduling
- reminders
- advice workflows
- planning
- utility-first output

### 8. Not a roleplay-only product
It may dramatize user situations, but it is not primarily a fictional roleplay system.
Its source world is the user’s own life.

### 9. Not an "AI for everything" platform
It should not become a broad miscellaneous AI toolbox.

## Section C — What is in scope

### In scope 1 — Companion-led entertainment experiences
This includes:
- Today’s Episode
- mood-based framing
- situation-based transformation
- interactive mini-dramas
- return hooks

### In scope 2 — Social entertainment wedge
This includes:
- Squad Mode
- friend or archetype setup
- group dynamics framing
- verdicts
- social entertainment outputs

### In scope 3 — Shareable artifacts
This includes:
- episode cards
- verdict cards
- squad result cards
- next-act cards
- entertainment summary artifacts

### In scope 4 — Continuity and memory
This includes:
- callbacks
- remembered style
- active hooks
- lightweight arcs
- visible continuity

### In scope 5 — Branded companion identity
This includes:
- stable tone
- recurring voice
- host/narrator presence
- recognizable product language

### In scope 6 — Founder-grade business framing
This includes:
- TAM
- adoption logic
- growth loops
- monetization path
- moat
- risks and mitigation

---

## Section D — What is out of scope

### Out of scope 1 — Therapy-first positioning
No support-first or emotionally heavy core product loop.

### Out of scope 2 — Full open-ended generic chat mode
A blank, unconstrained chat experience should not define the MVP.

### Out of scope 3 — Full social network infrastructure
No feed, comment graph, follower graph, or community platform in MVP.

### Out of scope 4 — Heavy gaming systems
No deep points economy, leaderboard backend, level design system, or reward economy in MVP.

### Out of scope 5 — Large creator ecosystem
No UGC platform or creator marketplace in MVP.

### Out of scope 6 — Production-scale backend complexity
No need to optimize for large-scale distributed infrastructure in early phases.

### Out of scope 7 — Too many entertainment modes
The MVP must stay sharply focused.

### Out of scope 8 — Advice-first or utility-first features
No study planner, productivity coach, or assistant-first functionality as core value.

### Out of scope 9 — Relationship-simulator direction
No AI girlfriend, AI spouse, or romance-centered positioning.

---

## Boundary summary
RANGMANCH is:
- an AI-native entertainment companion
- a life-to-entertainment transformation system
- a continuity-rich, socially alive product
- a short-session consumer entertainment experience
- a companion-led personal entertainment operating system

RANGMANCH is not:
- a therapist-first companion
- a chatbot clone
- a Character AI copy
- a social feed app
- a casual game only
- a meme tool only
- a productivity assistant
- a roleplay-only product

These boundaries are now locked for later phases.
