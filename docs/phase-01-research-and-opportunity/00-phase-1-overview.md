# 00 — Phase 1 Overview

## Phase title
**Phase 1 — Problem Research, Behavioral Insight, and Opportunity Discovery**

## Core objective
The purpose of Phase 1 is to build a serious, evidence-backed understanding of:
- user behavior
- entertainment patterns
- AI companion limitations
- mobile social habits
- and the opportunity space where RANGMANCH can win

## Why this phase exists
Without research depth, RANGMANCH risks sounding imaginative but unsupported.

Phase 1 exists to prove that the concept is not random invention. It must be grounded in:
- real behavior
- real gaps
- real usage patterns
- and reasoned insight

## The key questions this phase must answer
- What exact user moments does RANGMANCH enter?
- What emotional and behavioral drivers make it sticky?
- What do existing products fail to do?
- What kinds of social behavior make the product spreadable?
- What does personalization need to do to feel meaningful?
- What occasion-based spikes could strengthen adoption?
- What makes an AI-native entertainment product structurally stronger than a template-based one?

## Required outputs
1. Research Synthesis Report
2. User Moment Map
3. User Psychology Map
4. Opportunity Gap Matrix
5. Top Behavioral Insights Memo
6. References Appendix
7. Success Criteria and Failure Modes
8. Exit Gate
9. Phase 1 Signoff

## Phase 1 lock rule
The output of Phase 1 must justify the need for RANGMANCH more strongly than Phase 0 alone.
