# 01 — Phase 1 Research Plan

## Purpose of this document
This document defines the exact research structure for Phase 1. Its purpose is to ensure that research is systematic, insight-driven, and directly useful for later product decisions.

## Research goal
Build an evidence-backed understanding of:
- entertainment behavior
- AI companion limitations
- social sharing behavior
- self-relevance and identity expression
- occasion-driven spikes in engagement
- and AI-native product design principles

## The six research pillars

### Pillar A — Entertainment behavior research
Study:
- short-form entertainment behavior
- casual fun products
- shareable digital entertainment
- boredom behavior
- micro-session mobile usage
- post-class, post-work, late-night, and spare-time entertainment patterns

Goal:
Understand when and why users seek light fun.

### Pillar B — Companion product landscape research
Study:
- existing AI companion products
- adjacent categories
- chat-heavy product limitations
- emotional-intensity risks
- social shareability weaknesses
- repeatability weaknesses

Goal:
Define whitespace.

### Pillar C — Social expression and group energy research
Study:
- friend-group banter
- inside-joke formation
- screenshot behavior
- messaging and forwarding behavior
- playful group participation triggers
- socially portable content behavior

Goal:
Understand how RANGMANCH can grow socially.

### Pillar D — Personalization and identity behavior research
Study:
- self-relevance
- personalized entertainment stickiness
- identity expression through content
- reactions to "content about me / my squad / my life"

Goal:
Strengthen self-relevance logic.

### Pillar E — Occasion and event behavior research
Study:
- birthdays
- festivals
- sports nights
- campus events
- trips
- weekends
- idle downtime
- chaotic shared moments

Goal:
Find high-energy moments where the product can spike.

### Pillar F — AI-native product design research
Study:
- what makes a product truly AI-native
- structured outputs vs raw generation
- memory and continuity
- proactive intelligence
- orchestration vs template automation

Goal:
Ensure AI is integral to the product.

## Required Phase 1 outputs
- User Moment Map
- User Psychology Map
- Opportunity Gap Matrix
- Top Behavioral Insights Memo
- Research Synthesis Report
- References Appendix

## Research quality rule
Phase 1 research should not merely describe markets or list competitors. It must produce product-shaping insight.
