# Phase 1 — Problem Research, Behavioral Insight, and Opportunity Discovery

## Purpose
Phase 1 exists to build an evidence-backed understanding of:
- user behavior
- entertainment patterns
- AI companion limitations
- mobile social habits
- and the opportunity space where RANGMANCH can win

This phase gives the project research depth.

## Why this phase matters
The case brief explicitly rewards:
- originality
- creativity
- user empathy
- depth of research
- cross-sectoral thinking
- original insights uncovered

Phase 1 is therefore not optional polish. It is a core scoring area.

## What this phase must produce
By the end of Phase 1, the project should have:
- a Research Synthesis Report
- a User Moment Map
- a User Psychology Map
- an Opportunity Gap Matrix
- a Top Behavioral Insights Memo
- a References Appendix
- a formal exit gate and signoff

## Research pillars
1. Entertainment behavior research
2. Companion product landscape research
3. Social expression and group energy research
4. Personalization and identity behavior research
5. Occasion and event behavior research
6. AI-native product design research
